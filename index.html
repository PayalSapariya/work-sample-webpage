Financial Tracker - Work Sample

Project Overview

Financial Tracker is a student-focused budgeting application designed to help individuals efficiently manage their expenses. The app provides an intuitive interface, real-time financial tracking, and goal-setting features that enable students to plan and allocate their money effectively. This project follows a structured UX design process to ensure usability and effectiveness.

Problem Statement

Students often struggle with managing finances due to irregular income, unexpected expenses, and a lack of structured financial tracking. This difficulty can impact their academic performance and overall financial stability. The Financial Tracker app addresses these issues by providing an easy-to-use platform to track expenses, set budgets, and achieve financial goals.

Research & Discovery

To ensure that our app meets the needs of students, we conducted extensive research using user interviews and surveys. Our key findings include:

  • 70% of students struggle with financial planning due to unexpected expenses.
  • Many users prefer a mobile-friendly solution with real-time tracking.
  • Students need automated insights and goal-setting features.

These insights helped shape the features and functionality of the app, ensuring that it addresses real student pain points.

Wireframing & Prototyping

We created low-fidelity wireframes to experiment with different layouts and user flows. Our goal was to ensure a seamless navigation experience and clear financial data visualization. Through multiple iterations, we refined the wireframes based on usability testing results.

User Testing & Iteration

We conducted usability testing with students to refine our prototype. Some of the key feedback and improvements made include:

  • Dashboard Enhancements: Users wanted a clearer overview of their expenses, so we redesigned the layout for better visibility.
  • Split-Expense Feature: A new feature was added based on student feedback, allowing shared expenses to be easily divided.
  • Improved Accessibility: High-contrast colors and better navigation were implemented to enhance usability for all users.

Final Solution & Impact

The final version of the Financial Tracker app integrates intuitive navigation, real-time financial tracking, and personalized goal management. After implementation:

  • 70% of students reported a reduction in financial stress.
  • Users found the app more effective than traditional budget tracking methods.
  • The split-expense feature received positive feedback, helping students manage shared costs.

The design process ensured that the app meets both functional and usability requirements, making financial management easier for students.
